# Plan Limits & Seats

A **seat** is a billable workspace member. On paid plans you're charged per seat, per billing cycle. Manage seats from Settings → Members.

**Member and project limits by plan:**

- **Starter (Free):** up to 5 members and 3 active projects.
- **Pro:** up to 50 members and unlimited projects.
- **Business:** unlimited members and unlimited projects.
- **Enterprise:** unlimited, with volume pricing.

**Guests** (view-only collaborators outside your team) don't count as seats. Pro includes up to 10 guests; Business and Enterprise include unlimited guests.

**Adding seats:** when you invite a member beyond your current seat count, the new seat is added immediately and prorated for the rest of the current billing cycle.

**Removing seats:** when you remove a member, their seat is freed at your next renewal, so you keep the capacity you've already paid for until then. Your renewal is then billed at the lower seat count.

If you hit a plan limit — for example a 6th member on Starter — Nimbus will prompt you to upgrade rather than blocking your work.
