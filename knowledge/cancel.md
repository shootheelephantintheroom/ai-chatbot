# Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel a paid Nimbus subscription at any time. A workspace owner or billing admin can do it from Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription.

What happens when you cancel:

- You keep full access to your paid features until the **end of the current billing period**. Cancelling stops the next renewal — it doesn't cut you off immediately.
- When the period ends, the workspace automatically moves to the **Starter (Free)** plan. Nothing is deleted; anything above Starter's limits becomes read-only until you're back under them or upgrade again.
- We don't pro-rate or refund the remainder of a period you've already paid for. (See the refund policy for the first-subscription money-back guarantee.)

On annual plans, cancelling stops the renewal at the end of your paid term; you keep access for the rest of that year.

Your data is retained after cancellation so you can reactivate later. If you want it removed, request deletion from Settings → Data & Privacy; we recommend exporting first. We permanently delete cancelled-workspace data 12 months after the plan ends unless you ask sooner.
