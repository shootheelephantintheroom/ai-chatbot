# Reset Your Password

To reset your Nimbus password:

1. Go to the login page at app.nimbus.com/login.
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form.
3. Enter the email address on your account and submit.
4. Open the reset email and click the link. The link is valid for 60 minutes.
5. Choose a new password (at least 10 characters) and sign in.

If your team uses single sign-on (SSO/SAML), Nimbus doesn't store your password — reset it through your identity provider (for example Okta or Google Workspace), then sign in with the SSO button.

**Didn't get the email?** Check your spam folder, confirm you entered the address you signed up with, and make sure no typos crept in. Reset emails can take a few minutes. If it still hasn't arrived after 15 minutes, request another link or contact support — we can trigger a reset manually.

For security, password reset links can only be used once and expire after 60 minutes.
