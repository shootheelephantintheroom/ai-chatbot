# Refund Policy

Nimbus offers a money-back guarantee on your first paid subscription. If you're on a monthly plan, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial charge. On an annual plan, the window is 30 days from the initial charge.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Once approved, they typically take 5–10 business days to appear, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Renewal charges (the second and later billing cycles) are generally non-refundable, because you can cancel at any time before a renewal to avoid being charged. That said, if you were charged for a renewal you didn't intend — for example, you meant to cancel but missed the date — contact support and we'll review it case by case.

We don't pro-rate refunds for partial billing periods. If you cancel mid-cycle, you keep access until the end of the period you already paid for.

To request a refund, reach out to support with the email on your account and the approximate charge date.
