# Pricing & Plans

Nimbus has four plans: Starter, Pro, Business, and Enterprise.

**Starter** is free forever. It includes one workspace, up to 3 active projects, up to 5 members, 100 MB of file storage, and community support. It's meant for individuals and small teams trying Nimbus out.

**Pro** is $12 per user / month billed monthly, or $10 per user / month billed annually. It adds unlimited projects, up to 50 members, 10 GB of storage per user, all standard integrations, and priority email support.

**Business** is $22 per user / month billed monthly, or $18 per user / month billed annually. It includes everything in Pro plus advanced permissions, SSO/SAML, audit logs, 100 GB of storage per user, and priority support with a 1-business-day response SLA.

**Enterprise** is custom-priced for large organizations. It adds a dedicated customer success manager, custom contracts and invoicing, unlimited storage, and a 99.9% uptime SLA. Contact sales for a quote.

All paid plans are billed per active seat. You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time from Settings → Billing; switching to annual applies the discounted rate at your next renewal.
